How To Play :



    -The aim of the nibbler is to get the most apple in the map.
     -If the nibbler touch one wall or one sections of his tails, the game is over.
     -If the tail of the nibbler fill all the map. The game is over and you win.




Choose the map of the nibbler :



    -Press E to load and play with the easy map.
     -Press H to load and play with the hard map.
